FREQUENTLY ASKED

Everything you're wondering about

Do I really need a VPN when I travel?+

If you connect to any public WiFi — airport, hotel, café — a VPN encrypts that connection so others on the same network can't see your traffic. It also helps keep your apps and streaming behaving the way they do at home.

Is using a VPN abroad legal?+

VPNs are legal in the large majority of countries. A small number of countries restrict or ban them, so it's worth checking local rules before you travel there.

Will a VPN slow down my internet?+

There's a small speed cost from encryption, but both NordVPN and Surfshark are built to keep that impact minimal on nearby servers.

NordVPN or Surfshark — which should I pick?+

Choose NordVPN if speed and server coverage matter most to you. Choose Surfshark if you're traveling with several devices or want the lower-cost option.
